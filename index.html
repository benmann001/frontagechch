Practical details

We help you navigate approvals, install and content.

Every location is different. We help consider landlord approval, shopping centre rules, signage requirements, electrical installation, product suitability and site visibility before you commit.

Can people still see through the window?

Yes. Transparent LED displays are designed to show digital content while preserving visibility through the glass. The exact result depends on the product, brightness, pixel pitch and installation type.

Will it work in daylight?

Brightness and placement matter. We assess sunlight exposure, viewing distance and the direction your window faces before recommending a display type.

Can I update the content myself?

Yes. Content is typically managed through a compatible control system. We can also provide monthly content support if you would rather have campaigns created for you.

Do I need landlord or Council approval?

It depends on your site, content, location and installation. We help identify the likely approval considerations before installation.
